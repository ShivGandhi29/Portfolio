Project Overview

Detour is a React Native iOS app (TypeScript, Expo, Xcode) that I designed, developed, and deployed to the App Store. As Tech Lead of a 4-person development team, I oversaw all phases of the SDLC including design, development, testing, deployment, and ongoing iteration. The app features social interactions, geolocation functionality, real-time updates, and monetization through Google AdMob and RevenueCat integration.
